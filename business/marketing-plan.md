# Marketing Plan for glm-forge
## Positioning
### Primary Positioning
1-line: "glm-forge: The industry's most scalable and efficient large language model for machine learning developers and researchers."

### Alternative Positioning
1. "Unlock the full potential of your machine learning projects with glm-forge, the ultimate large language model solution."
2. "glm-forge: Revolutionizing the way you work with large language models, tailored for the needs of machine learning developers and researchers."
3. "Effortlessly integrate large language models into your workflows with glm-forge, the go-to solution for machine learning professionals."

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
### Persona 1: Alex Chen, ML Engineer
- Daily Job: Developing and deploying large language models for a Fortune 500 company
- Budget: $10,000 - $50,000 per year for software and services
- Pain Points: Scalability, efficiency, and ease of use when working with large language models

### Persona 2: Dr. Maria Rodriguez, Research Scientist
- Daily Job: Conducting research on natural language processing and machine learning
- Budget: $5,000 - $20,000 per year for software and services
- Pain Points: Access to powerful and efficient large language models for research purposes

### Persona 3: Jack Lee, Data Scientist
- Daily Job: Building and maintaining machine learning models for a mid-sized company
- Budget: $2,000 - $10,000 per year for software and services
- Pain Points: Limited resources and expertise when working with large language models

## Channels
### Primary Channels
1. **Content Marketing**: Estimated CAC: $50 - $100 per lead
	* Blog posts, articles, and guides on large language models and machine learning
	* Guest posts on popular industry blogs
2. **Social Media**: Estimated CAC: $20 - $50 per lead
	* Twitter, LinkedIn, and Reddit presence for glm-forge
	* Engaging with industry professionals and thought leaders
3. **Email Marketing**: Estimated CAC: $10 - $20 per lead
	* Newsletters and updates on glm-forge features and developments
	* Exclusive content and resources for subscribers

## Content Cadence
### Week 1-4: Awareness and Education
- Publish 2 blog posts on large language models and machine learning
- Share 4 social media posts on Twitter, LinkedIn, and Reddit
- Send 1 email newsletter with updates on glm-forge

### Week 5-8: Product Showcase
- Publish 2 case studies on glm-forge use cases
- Share 4 social media posts on product features and benefits
- Send 1 email newsletter with product updates

### Week 9-12: Thought Leadership
- Publish 2 whitepapers on large language models and machine learning
- Share 4 social media posts on industry trends and insights
- Send 1 email newsletter with thought leadership content

## Success Metrics

- Target: 100 DAU within the first 30 days
- Metric: Track DAU growth and engagement metrics

### $MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) Target
- Target: $10,000 MRR within the first 90 days
- Metric: Track MRR growth and customer acquisition costs
