# Partner Targets
## Introduction
To further enhance the capabilities of glm-forge and expand its reach, we have identified key partners for integration. These partnerships will not only increase the value proposition of our product but also open up new revenue streams through affiliate and revenue-sharing models.

## Partner Integration Roadmap
The following SaaS/API integrations are prioritized based on their potential to solve specific user jobs, their free-tier limits, the effort required for integration (S/M/L), and the potential for affiliate or revenue-share opportunities.

### 1. **Hugging Face**
- **Integration Effort:** M
- **Free-Tier Limits:** 100,000 requests per day
- **Value-Add:** Simplifies the deployment and management of large language models like GLM-5 for machine learning developers and researchers.
- **Rationale:** Hugging Face's Transformers library is widely used in the machine learning community. Integrating glm-forge with Hugging Face's model hub would allow users to easily deploy and manage GLM-5 models, enhancing the user experience and opening up potential revenue streams through Hugging Face's model sharing and deployment services.

### 2. **Google Cloud AI Platform**
- **Integration Effort:** L
- **Free-Tier Limits:** $300 credit for new users
- **Value-Add:** Enables scalable deployment and management of large language models on Google Cloud, solving the infrastructure scalability problem for users.
- **Rationale:** Google Cloud AI Platform offers a robust infrastructure for deploying and managing machine learning models. Integrating glm-forge with Google Cloud AI Platform would provide users with a scalable solution for deploying GLM-5 models, with potential for revenue sharing through Google Cloud's partner program.

### 3. **AWS SageMaker**
- **Integration Effort:** L
- **Free-Tier Limits:** 12 months free for new users, with some limitations
- **Value-Add:** Streamlines the process of building, training, and deploying machine learning models, including large language models like GLM-5, on AWS.
- **Rationale:** AWS SageMaker is a leading platform for machine learning model development and deployment. Integrating glm-forge with AWS SageMaker would enhance the development and deployment experience for users, with potential revenue streams through AWS's partner network.

### 4. **Weights & Biases**
- **Integration Effort:** S
- **Free-Tier Limits:** Limited to 100 experiments
- **Value-Add:** Provides a platform for tracking and visualizing machine learning experiments, including those involving large language models like GLM-5.
- **Rationale:** Weights & Biases offers a popular platform for machine learning experiment tracking. Integrating glm-forge with Weights & Biases would help users monitor and optimize their GLM-5 model training processes, with potential for affiliate marketing opportunities.

### 5. **Kaggle**
- **Integration Effort:** M
- **Free-Tier Limits:** Unlimited for public datasets and competitions
- **Value-Add:** Offers a community-driven platform for hosting datasets, competitions, and notebooks related to machine learning, including large language models.
- **Rationale:** Kaggle is a well-known platform in the machine learning community. Integrating glm-forge with Kaggle would allow users to share and discover GLM-5 related datasets and competitions, enhancing community engagement and potentially generating revenue through Kaggle's competition and dataset hosting services.

### 6. **Streamlit**
- **Integration Effort:** S
- **Free-Tier Limits:** Unlimited for public apps
- **Value-Add:** Enables the creation of web apps for data science and machine learning models, including demos and interfaces for large language models like GLM-5.
- **Rationale:** Streamlit provides an easy-to-use platform for creating web apps for machine learning models. Integrating glm-forge with Streamlit would allow users to easily create and share web demos of their GLM-5 models, with potential for affiliate marketing.

### 7. **GitHub**
- **Integration Effort:** M
- **Free-Tier Limits:** Unlimited public repositories
- **Value-Add:** Enhances collaboration and version control for machine learning projects, including those involving large language models like GLM-5.
- **Rationale:** GitHub is the leading platform for version control and collaboration in software development. Integrating glm-forge with GitHub would streamline the development process for users working on GLM-5 projects, with potential for revenue through GitHub's marketplace and actions services.

### 8. **Notion**
- **Integration Effort:** S
- **Free-Tier Limits:** Limited to 100 blocks
- **Value-Add:** Provides a workspace for notes, documentation, and project management, useful for organizing machine learning projects involving large language models.
- **Rationale:** Notion offers a versatile workspace for notes, documentation, and project management. Integrating glm-forge with Notion would help users organize their GLM-5 projects and documentation, with potential for affiliate marketing opportunities.

Each of these integrations is designed to enhance the user experience, solve specific pain points, and open up new revenue streams for glm-forge. Prioritizing these integrations based on their value-add, integration effort, and potential for affiliate or revenue-share opportunities will ensure that we maximize the impact of our partnership strategy.
