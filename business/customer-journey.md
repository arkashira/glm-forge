# Customer Journey Map for glm-forge

## Aware Phase

### Trigger Event
- Researchers and developers become aware of the need for a scalable and efficient large language model like GLM-5.
- They search online for solutions and come across glm-forge.

### Friction Points
- Difficulty in finding a suitable large language model that meets their needs.
- Limited knowledge about the capabilities and limitations of large language models.

### User Emotions
- Frustration
- Confusion

### Opportunities to Delight
- Provide clear and concise documentation about the benefits and features of glm-forge.
- Offer a free trial or demo to showcase the capabilities of the model.

### Metric per Phase
- Awareness Rate (number of people aware of glm-forge)
- 20% of researchers and developers in the machine learning community are aware of glm-forge within the first 6 months.

## Consider Phase

### Trigger Event
- Researchers and developers consider glm-forge as a potential solution to their needs.
- They evaluate the pros and cons of using glm-forge.

### Friction Points
- Limited information about the scalability and efficiency of glm-forge.
- Concerns about the compatibility of glm-forge with their existing workflows.

### User Emotions
- Hope
- Uncertainty

### Opportunities to Delight
- Provide case studies or success stories of researchers and developers who have used glm-forge.
- Offer a free consultation or demo to address their concerns.

### Metric per Phase
- Consideration Rate (number of people considering glm-forge)
- 30% of researchers and developers who are aware of glm-forge consider it as a potential solution within the first 3 months.

## Try Phase

### Trigger Event
- Researchers and developers decide to try glm-forge.
- They sign up for a free trial or demo.

### Friction Points
- Difficulty in setting up and integrating glm-forge with their existing workflows.

### User Emotions
- Excitement
- Frustration

### Opportunities to Delight

- Offer dedicated support or resources to help them get started.

### Metric per Phase
- Trial Rate (number of people trying glm-forge)
- 40% of researchers and developers who consider glm-forge try it within the first 2 months.
